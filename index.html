about sicet

Sri Indu College of Engineering and Technology, Hyderabad established in 2001, is affiliated to JNTUH, Hyderabad and approved by AICTE. The college offers UG and PG courses in the discipline of Engineering & Technology and Management across various specializations, approved by UGC. The JEE Mains or AP-EAMCET entrance exam is compulsory for admission to UG level engineering and technology courses at Sri Indu College of Engineering and Technology. Institute has an active relationship with various companies for placement assistance. In 2020-21, around 696 students were successfully recruited across various renowned companies.
